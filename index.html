The Bigger Picture

Two arms of the same project.

The Windstorm Institute publishes research on the mathematical constraints — information-theoretic and thermodynamic — that govern what physical systems can do. Windstorm Labs builds the AI tools and infrastructure that let those constraints get operationalized into shipping products. The same hardware that runs the autonomous research agents also serves the Windy product backends. The same person founded both. The two organizations are independent in legal status (the Institute is research-coded; Labs is a Utah LLC shipping products) but unified in mission: build infrastructure that respects the limits the Institute discovers, and use that infrastructure to make new tools possible.
